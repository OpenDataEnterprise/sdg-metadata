15
  <p>In formula:</p>
16
+ <p>A<sub>we</sub> = (GVA<sub>a</sub> x (1-C<sub>r</sub>))/V<sub>a</sub></p>

  <p>Where:</p>
18

21
  <p>V<sub>a</sub> = Volume of water used by the agricultural sector (including irrigation, livestock and aquaculture) [m<sup>3</sup>]</p>
22

24
