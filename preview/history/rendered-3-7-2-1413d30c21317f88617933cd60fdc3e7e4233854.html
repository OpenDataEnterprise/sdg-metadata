4.f. Treatment of missing values (i) at country level and (ii) at regional level (IMPUTATION)

Treatment of missing values:

  • At country level:

There is no attempt to provide estimates for individual countries or areas when country or area data are not available.

  • At regional and global levels:

The regional or global aggregates of the adolescent birth rate for the age group 15-19 years are from the latest revision of World Population Prospects (WPP) produced by the Population Division. In cases where data reviewing and weighting each observation analytically, or, in more recent years, using automated statistical methods, or by using a bias-adjusted data model to control for systematic biases between different types of data.
