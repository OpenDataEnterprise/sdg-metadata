  <h2>Computation method:</h2>
3
+ <p>Youth NEET rate =</p>
4
+ <p>(Youth &#x2013; (Youth in employment + Youth not in employment but in education or training))/Youth) x 100</p>
5
  <p>It is important to note here that youth simultaneously in employment and education or training should not be double counted when subtracted from the total number of youth.</p>
6
  <p>The formula can also be expressed as:</p>
7
+ <p>Youth NEET rate =</p>
8
+ <p>((Unemployed youth + Youth outside the labour force) &#x2013; (Unemployed youth in education or training + Youth outside the labour force in education or training)) Youth x 100</p>
