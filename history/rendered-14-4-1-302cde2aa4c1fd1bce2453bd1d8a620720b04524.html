4.f. Treatment of missing values (i) at country level and (ii) at regional level (IMPUTATION)

Treatment of missing values:

  • At country level:

No interpolation is carried out for missing data.

  • At regional and global levels:

A fixed number of fish stocks is monitored and assessed in terms of their stock.
